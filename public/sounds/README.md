# Sound Notification Files

Place your notification sound files here:

- `warn.mp3` - Played for warning-level alerts
- `critical.mp3` - Played for critical-level alerts

## Recommended Sounds

Use subtle, non-intrusive sounds that:
- Are short (< 2 seconds)
- Have a calm, professional tone
- Are not startling or alarming

## Free Sound Resources

- [Notification Sounds](https://notificationsounds.com/)
- [Freesound.org](https://freesound.org/)
- [Mixkit](https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/notification/)

## Default Behavior

Sound notifications are **disabled by default**. Users must explicitly enable them in Settings.
